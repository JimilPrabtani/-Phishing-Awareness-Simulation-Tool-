What Happened?

You clicked a link in a simulated phishing email. In a real attack, this could have led to:

  • 🔑 A fake login page stealing your credentials
  • 💾 Malware being downloaded to your device
  • 🏢 An attacker gaining access to company systems

🛡️ How to Spot Phishing

  1. Check the sender — Is it from a legitimate domain?
  2. Hover over links — Does the URL match what's displayed?
  3. Look for urgency — "Act NOW" is a red flag
  4. Verify separately — Call IT or visit the real website directly
  5. Check for errors — Typos and odd formatting are common in phishing

✅ Remember: If you ever suspect a real phishing email, report it immediately to your IT Security team. You are the first line of defense!
